Dum Pukht

Royal Indian Fine Dining

“The art of slow cooking, perfected over generations”

Nestled in the heart of New Delhi, Dum Pukht brings alive the royal Awadhi tradition of slow-cooked, aromatic cuisine. Our chefs honour centuries-old recipes passed down through Nawabi kitchens, creating dishes that are sealed and cooked on a slow fire — the ancient art of dum.
